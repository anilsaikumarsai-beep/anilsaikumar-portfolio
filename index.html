Our Story

From a small workshop to the world's stages

FluteCraft was born in 1978 when master craftsman Venkataraman Pillai, inspired by the classical music tradition of Madurai, began carving bamboo flutes in a small workshop near the Meenakshi temple.

What started as a humble endeavour driven by devotion to music has grown into one of India's most respected flute manufacturers. Today, his grandchildren continue the tradition — blending ancestral technique with modern acoustic science.

Our flutes are played on concert stages across 38 countries, in orchestras from Chennai to Vienna, in music schools from Mumbai to Tokyo. Yet every instrument is still made one at a time, by hand, with the same care and reverence as that first flute in 1978.
